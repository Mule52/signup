SigningDay is a lacrosse-recruiting platform that puts players in control of the recruiting process. On SigningDay, players manage their own recruiting profiles, post first-hand content and communicate directly with college coaches. SigningDay provides athletes with the tools and resources to achieve success in the recruiting process and throughout their careers.

SigningDay Lacrosse is a three-step process.

  1. Choose an evaluation package
  2. Fill out parent and player profile information
  3. Pay for the evaluation package and await email confirmation
